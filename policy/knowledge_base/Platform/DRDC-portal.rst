Purpose
-------
This section describes the DRDC portal that's available for DevOps teams to use DRCP.

.. note:: | Why do you need the DRDC portal for DRCP?
  | DRCP integrates with the existing `DRDC <https://confluence.office01.internalcorp.net:8453/display/DRDCKB/DRDC+knowledge+base>`__ portal which was already available within APG for Landing zone 2. In the future, the name of this portal might change.
