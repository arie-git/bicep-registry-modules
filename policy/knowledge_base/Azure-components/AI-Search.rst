AI Search
=========

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1
   :glob:

   AI-Search/*

Azure AI Search (formerly known as "Azure Cognitive Search") is a service offering advanced search capabilities, including full-text, filtering, and AI-driven cognitive search. It also supports vector search for semantic search scenarios, enabling more relevant results by matching the meaning and context of queries rather than relying solely on keywords.
