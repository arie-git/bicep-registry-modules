Notification Hubs
=================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1
   :glob:

   Notification-Hubs/*

Azure Notification Hubs is a cloud-based push notification service that allows businesses to send personalized and targeted notifications to millions of users across platforms. With high scalability and low latency, Notification Hubs ensures fast and reliable delivery of messages, regardless of the device or platform.

By leveraging Notification Hubs, businesses can engage users in real-time with alerts, updates, and promotions, enhancing customer engagement, retention, and experience.
