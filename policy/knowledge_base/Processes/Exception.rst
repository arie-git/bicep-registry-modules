Exception process
=================

| Within DRCP Landing zone 3 - exceptions follow the standard APG exception process described here: `Exception process Central APG Policies <https://cloudapg.sharepoint.com/sites/TeamAPG-DigiSquare/SitePages/en/Exceptieproces.aspx>`__. Find the Dutch version `here <https://cloudapg.sharepoint.com/sites/TeamAPG-DigiSquare/SitePages/en/Exceptieproces.aspx>`__.

Follow-up after exception request
---------------------------------

| After the preceding mentioned process, the DevOps team needs to forward the agreement of the DHT, including the exception form, towards the Product Owner of DRCP.
| The PO will then register the exception in ServiceNow.
| See page :doc:`DRCP exceptions <../Platform/Policies-controls-and-exemptions/DRCP-exceptions>` how an exception can be technically applied in case it's related to a DRCP policy.
